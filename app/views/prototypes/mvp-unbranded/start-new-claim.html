Start a new claim

Create a new Flexible Support Fund (FSF) claim where the payment methods are:

  • Approval to Buy (A2B) or
  • Central Payment System (CPS)

And the customer is:

  • a Universal Credit claimant applying for FSF1 (Barriers Spend)
  • not claiming an In Work Emergency Payment

Eligibility requirements (opens in new tab)

Before you start

Before starting, make sure that you have verified the customer’s identity and have access to their:

  • National Insurance number
  • Universal Credit details

Where relevant, you will also need:

  • evidence supporting the reason for the claim
  • supplier and purchase details for all claimed items
  • training course or employer details
  • proof of purchase for retrospective payments
